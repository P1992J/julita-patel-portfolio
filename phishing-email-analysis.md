# Phishing Email Analysis — Blue Team Labs Online

**Platform:** Blue Team Labs Online
**Category:** Phishing Analysis
**Difficulty:** Easy
**Date:** 1 June 2026

## Scenario
A user received a phishing email and forwarded 
it to the SOC for investigation.

## Tools Used
- Mozilla Thunderbird
- whois.domaintools.com
- URL2PNG
- Manual header analysis

## Key Findings
- Recipient: kinnar1975@yahoo.co.uk
- Originating IP: 103.9.171.10
- Malicious URL: hosted on Google Blogger
- Email used contact form injection technique
- Verdict: Confirmed phishing/spam campaign

## Key Learnings
- How to read raw email headers
- How to identify originating IP addresses
- How to safely analyse malicious URLs
- How to identify phishing hosting platforms
